Send us your UUID

Your UUID is basically your Device ID, it is a 40 digit alpha-numeric code that represents your iPhone. We need it to install PicStroom on your iPad.

This are the steps to take:

  1. Open iTunes and connect your iPad
  2. Click “serial number” label. It will change to Identifier
  3. Choose from the file menu: Edit -> Copy. (This will put the device ID in your clipboard so you can paste it)
  4. Paste the device ID (Edit -> Paste from the file menu on top of your screen) in an email to support@picstroom.com.

We will reply promptly!
