Your favorite image sites in one view

PicStroom is a beautiful application making following images from sites easy and engaging.
Take your favourite websites and transform their published pictures into a "picture stream" that you can browse with truly amazing speed.
PicStroom will autodetect valid RSS feeds for you.

Tap on a picture to see it in full size or view the picture in its original website from within our embedded browser.
Save and share your images via Dropbox or email. View your own or shared Dropbox folders as "picture streams" in their own right..
